# Dashboard overview

The home page of the UI (`/`). Shows a live summary of the CA
infrastructure.

## Summary cards

Aggregated metrics at the top:

* **Certification Authorities** — total, root, and intermediate count.
* **Certificates** — active, revoked, expired.

Numbers are computed in real time from the database and updated on every
visit.

## Tables

* **Certificate Authorities** — list with issued / active / revoked counts.
* **Expiring soon** — certificates expiring in the next 30 days.
* **Recently issued** — last 10 certificates.
